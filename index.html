    Now show us your roofs

    This is the single most useful thing you can do in this assessment. The map should already be near your facility — if not, use search or GPS.

    1. Find your facility. Zoom in until you can see the buildings clearly.
    2. Drop the pin. When your facility is in the middle of the map, press "Pin my facility".
    3. Outline the roofs. Press "Draw a roof", then tap each corner of a roof. Press "Finish roof" when done. Repeat for every good roof — flat or gently sloping, not shaded by trees or towers.
    The map needs an internet connection and doesn't seem to be loading. You can still submit — press "Finish" below and SHIELD will locate your site with you by phone.
