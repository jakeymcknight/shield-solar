Could your hospital run on sunshine?

Find your facility on the satellite map, tap the corners of your roofs, and see in seconds how much solar they could carry. It takes about five minutes — and it's the most useful five minutes of this whole assessment.

  1. Find your facility. Search your town or hospital name, or paste GPS numbers, then zoom in until you can see the buildings clearly.
  2. Drop the pin. When your facility is in the middle of the map, press "Pin my facility".
  3. Outline the roofs. Press "Draw a roof", tap each corner, press "Finish roof". Repeat for every good roof — flat or gently sloping, not shaded by trees or towers.
The map needs an internet connection and doesn't seem to be loading. You can still continue — fill in the forms and SHIELD will map your site with you by phone.
